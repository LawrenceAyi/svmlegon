Small Animal Teaching Hospital (SATH)

The Small Animal Teaching Hospital offers a wide range of medical and surgical services to companion animals (dogs and cats). Our veterinarians are amongst the best, and they prioritize the health of your pet above anything else. We provide compassionate care that is worthy of your trust.

History

The Small Animal Teaching Hospital was established in 2015 but formally opened in June 2016 in response to Ghana Veterinary Council’s requirement for the School of Veterinary Medicine to have a teaching facility for clinical training of veterinary student.

Our Vision

To advance medicine by integrating outstanding animal health care with leadership in veterinary education and cutting-edge research.

Our Mission

We strive to educate future veterinarians through excellent, innovative veterinary care and service to our patients, clients, referring veterinarians and community at large. We seek to treat our key stakeholders with utmost respect, integrity and compassion

Our Mandate

The mandate of the SVM is to train and equip veterinary professionals to manage, control and prevent diseases of livestock, companion animals or pets, wildlife, or exotic animals and in addition, to prevent and control diseases which are transmissible from animals to humans.

Services We Offer

  • General Medical Examination
  • Consultation
  • Vaccination
  • Laboratory Services
  • Theriogenology
  • Ultrasound
  • General Surgery
  • Pet Identification (Micro Chipping)
  • Veterinary Pharmacy

Also available are a wide range of pet food and accessories
